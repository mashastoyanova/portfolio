This web page is a work-in-progress, and was designed to demonstrate how much (or how little) I have learned during the first month of "Creating History in the Digital Media" class. This is my second year in the Art History Graduate program at George Mason University, and my last semester of coursework before I start writing thesis. My research interests are:

  • eighteenth-century art in Europe
  • perception of Pompeian art in the eighteenth and nineteenth-century Russia
  • influence of the rediscovery of Pompeii on the Russian arts
  • cultural interconnections between Western and Eastern Europe
  • the Russians on the Grand Tour
  • Soviet art sales
